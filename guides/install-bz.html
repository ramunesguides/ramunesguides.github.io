Below Zero Guide
Step by step guide to setup mods on latest the version of Below Zero
  SMLHelper   QModManager   Version Checker
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Download the three mods above

Place the zip files into your game folder (click me)

Show image


Extract  QModManager_BZ.STABLE.zip  into your game folder

Show image


Launch the game to generate a  QMods  folder within your game folder

Show image


Move the  SMLHelper_BZ.STABLE.zip  and  VersionChecker_BELOWZERO.zip  zip files
to your  QMods  folder, then extract/unzip both

Show image


Delete any left over zip files

You're good to go now! Happy modding, and for any further
questions, you can just ask in the Subnautica Modding discord
